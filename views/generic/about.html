About

There's not too much to say about this comic or myself really but I'll try and add any background info I can just in case.

As far as I'm concerned most comics can suffice as one or four panels thanks to a childhood filled with The Far Side and Calvin and Hobbies. So majority of my comics will be in one of those two formats, sometimes a story may need a different amount yet that should be the norm here.

You'll also probably find some influences from The Far Side in my comics and for that I apologize for nothing. So, please enjoy my comics and if you like what I write please consider supporting the comic if you can at Patreon.
